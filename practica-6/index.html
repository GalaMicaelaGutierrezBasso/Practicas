Last Year

Last year I was having dinner with my friends and one of them, Martin, is a very well known chef at an amazing restaurant. We were talking about the best restaurant and foods. He told me that the other day he wanted to have Italian food, so he asked his coworkers what was the best Italian food place in the area. Someone recommended him a place called “Florencios house”, so he has recommended that to me and now Im sharing this with you.

You must be thinking “Why would I go there?” or maybe “I dont even know anything about this place”, then let me explain it to you and perhaps try to convince you to go.

Reasons why you should go there:

  • This place is a family company that sells the best homemade pizza with all the ingredients that you could think of.
  • Also the pasta there is so delicious and looks just like in the movies.
  • The place is so cozy and the people that work there are really friendly and funny.
  • When you enter they give you a little pin with your name so when they serve you they know your name and I think thats nice because it makes you feel like youre more than just a random person thats eating there.
  • And finally when youre leaving they take a picture with you and put it up on the wall with your name and where youre from.

I hope I convinced you to go and that you like the place as much as I do now.
